About Aegis Vault

Aegis Vault is a free, open-source desktop application that lets you encrypt and store files on Internet Archive with zero-knowledge encryption.

Features

  • AES-256 encryption — your files, your keys
  • Direct upload to Internet Archive
  • Batch upload and download support
  • Multiple provider URL downloads
  • Multi-threaded IDM-style downloads
  • Cross-platform (macOS & Windows)
